Skills & Expertise

Areas of specialization in creative marketing production

Creative Production Management

  • End-to-end production of advertising creatives: from idea and brief to launch and optimization.
  • Developing creative strategies for user-generated content (UGC), video, static banners, playables and interactive ad formats.
  • Communicate with creative and development teams (UI/UX designers, unity developers, motion designers, sound designers, level artists, animators) and external agencies.
  • Managing the creative process: writing briefs, conducting brainstorming sessions, overseeing timelines, and approving final assets.
  • Experience creating authentic advertising creatives that accurately reflect gameplay, filter out irrelevant users, and improve install quality.

Performance Marketing & Analytics (Data-Driven Marketing)

  • Deep understanding of key mobile marketing metrics: CPI, CPA, ROAS, LTV, CTR, IPM (Installs per Mille).
  • Hands-on experience with attribution platforms (AppsFlyer, Adjust) and analytics tools (BI).
  • Running A/B tests on creatives to optimize campaign performance.
  • Analyzing data to identify winning creatives (hero creatives) and determine the reasons behind their success.

Soft Skills

  • Leadership and cross-functional collaboration: acting as a bridge between marketing, product, and analytics departments.
  • Excellent communication skills for presenting creative strategy and data-driven rationale for decisions.
  • Critical thinking and ability to make quick decisions in a fast-paced environment.
  • Results-oriented approach with a deep understanding of business metrics.

Technical & Production Skills

  • Knowledge of video production and editing basics (working with editing timelines, basic proficiency in Adobe Premiere Pro/After Effects).
  • Understanding of ad network specifications and requirements (Facebook, Google UAC, TikTok, Unity LevelPlay, etc.).
  • Experience with project management tools (Jira, Asana, Trello).
  • Budget Planning
  • Risk Management
